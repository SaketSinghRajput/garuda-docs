Electrical notes 1. All 5V lines must be fused at 500mA per branch.
2. 12V input requires TVS diode (P6KE18A) for spike protection.
3. I2C pull-up: 4.7kΩ to 3.3V on SDA and SCL lines.
4. Camera CSI ribbon max length: 200mm — use shielded extension for longer runs.
5. USB cables must be data-rated (not charge-only) for camera operation.
Installation notes 1. Mount AI module on anti-vibration rubber standoffs (M3).
2. Route all wiring away from airbag deployment zones.
3. Driver camera angle: 15° downward tilt toward driver eyes.
4. GPS antenna must have clear sky view — avoid metallic roof.
5. Brake wire tap: connect only to brake light feed wire, not ground.
Safety & compliance 1. All modifications reversible — no permanent vehicle wiring changes.
2. BLE frequency: 2.402–2.480 GHz — compliant with BIS/WPC India.
3. OBD-II read-only — no ECU write access in current firmware.
4. Emergency SOS requires active SIM in 4G module.
5. Designed to AIS-140 telematics standard compatibility.
